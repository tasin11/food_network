Fruit

Fruits Contain Lots of Fiber, Vitamins, Minerals and Antioxidants. Of course, fruits are more than just watery bags of fructose. There are lots of nutrients in them that are important for health. This includes fiber, vitamins and minerals, as well as a plethora of antioxidants and other plant compounds.

Nougat

Nougat is a rich source of protein because it contains eggs and almonds, seven of the nine essential amino acids, essential fatty acids, carbohydrates and vitamins A, E, B1 and B2. In addition, almonds have a high energy value and provide minerals to the body, such as magnesium, iron and potassium.
